## Journal articles

**Sedimentation of a sphere near a deformable elastic boundary in a viscous fluid**
*J. M. E., A. N. Collaborator* · *Journal of Fluid Mechanics* · 2025 (under review)

We characterise the settling trajectory and terminal velocity of a rigid sphere
approaching a thin elastic membrane, identifying three regimes distinguished by
the elastohydrodynamic number.

**Curling dynamics of an elastic ribbon in low-Reynolds-number flow**
*J. M. E.* · preprint · 2024

A slender-body / Donnell shell framework for the deployment of a pre-curved
elastic ribbon subject to viscous drag forces.
